Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy explains how Roofsy (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you request a roof inspection, including when you submit information through a Facebook lead form or other online form.

Information We Collect

When you request a free roof inspection, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and any other information you provide (such as your address or notes about your property).

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to contact you about your requested roof inspection. That may include calling or emailing you to confirm your request, answer questions, and schedule an inspection with a roofing specialist.

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing With Service Providers

We may share your information only as needed with service providers or roofing specialists who help us fulfill your requested inspection. For example, we may share your contact details and property information so that a local specialist can schedule and perform your inspection.

Questions

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, you can contact us through the contact options provided on the Roofsy website.
